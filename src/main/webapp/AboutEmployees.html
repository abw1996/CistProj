Jason Maine


Advanced HairStylist

With over 25 years’ experience in Atlanta's most exclusive upscale salons, Jason Maine has developed a passion for a more relaxed and collaborative approach with his clients. Jason Maine articulates his vision, “I believe that beauty today is a team effort. No skill or hair style is more important than the client wearing it, and no look is complete if the client’s personal style is not considered. Today’s fashion savvy customer seeks skill, wear-ability and exceptional execution. Jason Maine began his career with an apprenticeship at Curl Me Crazy salon impresario, Donald Trammel’s, Leiben Salon. After honing his skills as a hair color specialist and master stylist, he was hand selected by Deborah to be one of 4 stylists to join his in his new venture, Claiborne’s Salon. Here Jason Maine had the pleasure of crafting new hair styles for such diverse A-list stars as Roger Daltrey, Mariel Hemmingway, Lil' Kim and Stevie Nicks among many others. While working with celebrities is always exciting, Jason Maine finds that he most enjoys working with the professional woman that possesses a strong sense of self and understanding of her own unique flair. Collaborating with his clients to create a beautiful expression of their personal style while incorporating a polished and sophisticated look, results in the ultimate realization of his artistry. Jason Maine specializes in natural highlights, color and color-correction. Matching color to the skin tone and vibrancy of the client while maintaining healthy, luxurious hair is his forte. Join his for a complimentary consultation to discover the ultimate expression of your unique beauty.

Michelle Banks


Cosmetologist

From the minute you join Michelle in her salon chair, you will feel at ease and ready to explore a fresh new take on your personal style. Michelle understands the importance of delivering a cut whose maintenance matches the client's lifestyle. He is passionate about her craft and realizing the outward expression of her client's personal sense of style. Michelle excels at helping clients articulate their vision for their look and hair care maintenance, then executing flawlessly, to deliver a finished product that exceeds expectations. Her colorist techniques include foil highlights, lowlights as well as multi-dimensional color. Michelle recognized her passion for all things beauty related at an early age. Her innate talents have benefited from a wide array of the nation's best hairstyle and beauty schools including Martin Parsons (Toronto), Toni and Guy (Dallas), Sebastian/Wella (Los Angeles), Modern Organic Products (Chicago), American Crew Men’s Academy (Atlanta), Redken 5th Avenue (New York) and Modern Organic to name a few.
